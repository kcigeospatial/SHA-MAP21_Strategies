Programs

  • Design-Build: An alternative project delivery method used to procure a single source contract with a Design-Build Team who is responsible for the completion of both the design and construction of a project. This allows projects to be delivered faster, reduce costs, and to assign the risk to the party best able to manage that risk.
  • CMAR: An alternative project delivery method in which a Contractor is procured utilizing the Competitive Sealed Proposal process to provide a range of preconstruction services to SHA during the design phase of a project. These services may include, but are not limited to, constructability analysis, value engineering, scheduling, site assessments, and cost estimating. The Contractor would act as a general contractor during the construction phase upon agreement with SHA to deliver the project at a Guaranteed Maximum Price.
