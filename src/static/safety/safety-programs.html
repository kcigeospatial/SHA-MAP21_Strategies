Driving Safely in Work Zones
You've seen the orange cones and barrels in Maryland's roadway work zones. You've probably seen our highway workers, too. They're out on the road every day mowing, repairing pot holes and repaiting roads and bridges - making our State safe for you and your family
Pedestrian and Bicycle Safety

Drive Safely Work Week
To help reduce motor vehicle crashes, the Maryland State Highway Administration (SHA) and partners are proud to join with the Network of Employers for Traffic Safety (NETS) in support of their annual Drive Safely Work Week (DSWW) recognition.
